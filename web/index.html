Reads the play

A goal, a red card, a hard odds move. Pitchwire turns the raw feed into one or two plain sentences a non-trader understands. It explains. It never tells you to bet.

Reads the market

Every read-out carries the feed's own sequence and timestamp. If the wire goes quiet, it says so. It never dresses a stale number up as live.

The predict game
